About

Redesigned Beam's Vehicle and User Management Tool

Role

Sole designer using Ant's design system as the foundations for the UI.

Period

Jun - Jul 2021

Challenge

Had a ton of requests for new features and improvements from ops team but time and dev resources were limited. Needed to prioritise the right issues while planning ahead for future additions.

Impact

Prioritised and redesigned core screens and flows within a month for a 20+ and growing ops team.

No event tracking implemented, as the tool served a small team, but steps required for commonly-used actions have been significantly reduced.
