About Suling

I started out in marketing where I worked directly with clients and customers to solve problems in hacky ways.

This experience led me to become a designer, where I could have a more direct impact on the product by identifying and solving problems more effectively.

I'm currently a Senior Product Designer with 6 years of experience building and optimising consumer products and internal tools.

I pride myself in my ability to adapt, having worked independently as the sole designer tackling complex workflows at Beam, leading discovery in mature problem spaces at Grab, and advocating for design quality at TikTok.
